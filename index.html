Projects

Tambayan ng Imus


This platform is a dedicated web application engineered specifically to serve as the central information hub for the student community of Cavite State University - Imus Campus. Developed with the modern student in mind, the website streamlines the delivery of critical campus-wide announcements, academic updates, event timelines, and student-led journalism. By aggregating vital university news into a single, reliable digital bulletin board, the project aims to eliminate communication gaps and ensure that the student body remains thoroughly informed and connected to campus life.

At the core of this project is a strong commitment to user experience, universal accessibility, and technical efficiency. The website features a fully responsive, mobile-first design, ensuring that students can easily navigate the interface and read articles whether they are browsing on a desktop at home or quickly checking their smartphones between classes. Furthermore, the platform is optimized for fast loading speeds to remain functional even under limited data or unstable campus Wi-Fi networks, ensuring that important information is delivered right when it matters most.

Hotel Innova


This project is a comprehensive hotel reservation and management system engineered to streamline daily hospitality operations and optimize overall guest administration. Developed as a robust web application, the system serves as a centralized platform designed to seamlessly handle room reservations, automate customer bookings, and maintain a dynamically updated database of room availability. By replacing manual scheduling with an automated workflow, the platform minimizes the risk of double-bookings and provides front-desk personnel with real-time visibility into occupied, vacant, and maintenance-pending rooms.

From a technical standpoint, the project serves as a practical demonstration of advanced backend logic and relational database integration. The underlying architecture is structured to securely process and store guest records, billing information, and transactional histories while maintaining high data integrity. By implementing complex queries, automated availability checks, and core hotel management functions, the application showcases how structured database management can be leveraged to build scalable, secure, and highly efficient enterprise solutions.

Tech Used: Computer

Fries Advertisement Design


This project is a graphical advertisement design campaign specifically conceptualized to promote a food product through highly engaging visual storytelling centered around french fries. Developed with a keen eye for marketing aesthetics, the project explores the intersection of consumer psychology and modern digital design principles to transform a simple comfort food into a compelling visual brand. Every layout is deliberately structured to capture the viewer's attention instantly, utilizing dynamic compositions and focal points that highlight the texture, warmth, and appetite appeal of the product.

Central to the success of this design project is a meticulous execution of color theory, strategic typography, and contemporary branding techniques. The visual strategy relies on a carefully selected color palette—incorporating rich, warm tones that naturally stimulate hunger, balanced against contrasting backgrounds to make the imagery pop. Complementing the color scheme is a thoughtful typographic hierarchy, pairing bold, energetic display fonts for punchy headlines with clean, readable body text for promotional details. By harmonizing these design elements, the final marketing presentation effectively communicates quality and flavor, demonstrating how professional graphic design can elevate a brand and drive consumer engagement.
