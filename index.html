Tambayan ng Imus


Tambayan ng Imus is a dedicated web application engineered specifically to serve as a central information hub and digital blog for the student community of Cavite State University - Imus Campus. The platform streamlines and consolidates the delivery of critical campus-wide announcements, academic updates, event timelines, and student-led journalism into a single, organized feed. Built with a responsive, mobile-first design and optimized for fast loading speeds, it allows students to easily navigate the interface and read articles whether they are browsing on a desktop at home or quickly checking their smartphones using limited mobile data between classes.

The system solves the prevalent issue of fragmented communication and information overload on campus, where vital university updates are often scattered across various social media groups, unofficial pages, or physical bulletin boards. By aggregating all essential news into one reliable repository, the project eliminates communication gaps and prevents students from missing important deadlines or announcement alerts. Furthermore, its technical focus on accessibility and lightweight performance directly solves the challenge of unstable campus Wi-Fi and limited data connectivity, ensuring that critical information remains universally reachable for every student when it matters most.

Tech Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Hotel Innova


Hotel Innova is a comprehensive hotel reservation and management system designed to automate daily hospitality operations and optimize overall guest administration. Operating as a centralized web application, the system seamlessly processes room reservations, handles customer check-ins and check-outs, and secures payment transactions. It features a dynamically updated dashboard that tracks room availability in real time, utilizing structured database management to instantly log and categorize rooms as occupied, vacant, or pending maintenance for front-desk personnel.

The system directly solves the inefficiency, human error, and high operational risks associated with manual scheduling and fragmented booking records in the hospitality industry. By enforcing automated backend verification, Hotel Innova completely eliminates the issue of double-bookings and scheduling conflicts. Furthermore, it addresses data security and disorganization concerns by replacing vulnerable paperwork with a secure, centralized relational database, ensuring that sensitive guest profiles, billing summaries, and transaction histories are organized, legally compliant, and protected against data loss.

Tech Used: Visual Studio Code, MySQL, Java, JFrame,

Fries Advertisement Design


This project is a graphical advertisement campaign engineered to promote a food brand through highly engaging visual storytelling centered around french fries. It utilizes a meticulous combination of color theory, deliberate typographic hierarchy, and modern layout compositions to create an eye-catching marketing presentation. By pairing bold, energetic display fonts with a warm, hunger-stimulating color palette, the campaign highlights the texture, freshness, and appetite appeal of the product across various digital promotional formats.

The project solves the challenge of consumer ad-fatigue and low brand engagement in a highly competitive fast-food market where basic product photos often fail to capture attention. By applying consumer psychology and strategic design principles rather than relying on plain imagery, it transforms a common comfort food into a compelling visual brand that stands out on digital feeds. Ultimately, it addresses the problem of ineffective marketing by demonstrating how professional, harmonized graphic elements can instantly capture a viewer's attention, communicate quality, and drive customer desire.

Tech Used: Canva
